What we do

Built for travel professionals who need a ground partner they can trust.

We specialize in Hida Takayama and the surrounding Gifu region — an area with extraordinary depth that most tour operators don't have local access to.

Our team handles every aspect of the on-ground experience: from itinerary design and supplier relationships to guide arrangement, logistics, and real-time support during the program.

Custom itinerary design
We build programs around your clients' interests — from single half-day experiences to multi-day curated itineraries across the region.
Private activity booking
Access to our full portfolio of private experiences — all running exclusively for your client's group.
Local guide arrangement
English-speaking guides with deep local knowledge — not agency staff, but people who live and work in Takayama.
Ground handling & logistics
Transport coordination, hotel liaison, and on-the-day support. We're reachable throughout the program.
Agent tariffs
Net rates available on request. We work with a clear, consistent pricing structure designed for professional partners.
