Our story

Founded with a single conviction: that the most valuable thing a traveller can bring home is a genuine human and cultural connection.

We work with local families, craftspeople, tea masters, and farmers who open their homes and workshops — not because it’s convenient, but because they believe in sharing what makes this place extraordinary.

Based in Takayama’s historic Old Town. Every experience runs exclusively for your group.

Our Philosophy

Most people visit Takayama and see the streets.
We hope they leave knowing the people.

Each experience begins with a question: what would we share with someone we care about? Not the highlights, but everyday life.

Through these shared moments, we hope you discover something new — about the culture, the people, and perhaps even yourself.
